# Student-Finance-Tracker

This project is a student finance tracker designed to help students manage their finances effectively. It allows users to track their income, expenses, and savings, providing insights into their financial habits and helping them make informed decisions about their money.

## Features

- **Set Budget**: Define your total budget with multiple currency options (USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, INR)
- **Add Expenses**: Track expenses by category or custom name
- **Real-time Balance**: Automatically calculates remaining balance
- **Expense Categories**: Pre-defined categories (Food, Transport, Entertainment, Shopping, Bills) or custom entries
- **Expense List**: View all your expenses in an organized list

## How to Use

1. **Select Currency**: Choose your preferred currency from the dropdown
2. **Set Budget**: Enter your total budget amount and click "Set Budget"
3. **Add Expenses**: 
   - Select a category from the dropdown or choose "Other" for custom entries
   - Enter the cost of the product
   - Click "Check Amount" to add the expense
4. **Manage Expenses**: Use edit or delete icons to modify your expense list
